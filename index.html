This writing tool is inspired by the Movie Plot Generator book, and is a fun and effective way to generate script ideas of your own. Check out the Idea Generator to create an outlandish logline to start with, or create one on your own by going straight to the Sketchpad. Inspired by the 7-Step Brainstorming Technique by Script Magazine's Jeanne Veillette Bowerman, in 2 hours you can take your logline to a fleshed-out idea, save it to your account, or save it as a PDF so you can start writing it out as a script. I hope you get a good laugh or a good dose of inspiration using this writing tool!
